<h2>Features</h2>



<p>The Stagg EKG is chock full of features that we think make it an amazing pour-over kettle for the home barista or coffee enthusiast.</p>



<h3>Temperature Adjustment</h3>



<p>To start, one of the best things about this kettle is that it has temperature selection. The knob on the corner of the base is used to adjust the temperature from 135 F to 212 F (57-100 C). We love this adjustability because it lets us brew everything from <a href="https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/equipment/brewing/11-best-ways-to-brew-coffee/">AeroPress to pour-over coffee</a> without losing cup quality by scalding the beans.</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image"><img src="https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-adjusting-temp-1024x735.jpg" alt="Adjusting the Temperature on the Stagg EKG" class="wp-image-629" srcset="https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-adjusting-temp-1024x735.jpg 1024w, https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-adjusting-temp-300x215.jpg 300w, https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-adjusting-temp-768x551.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" /></figure>



<h3>Brew Stopwatch</h3>



<p>The kettle has a built-in brew timer that works like a charm. The temperature adjustment knob also doubles as a button to start the brew timer. When youre ready to start it, you simply push and hold the knob down for 2 seconds. To stop the timer, you press the knob down one more time. We like this feature because timing your pour overs is one of the easiest ways to increase repeatability between cups.</p>



<h3>Temperature Conversion</h3>



<p>The Stagg EKG base is capable of switching between Fahrenheit and Celsius at the drop of a hat. All it takes is toggling the F/C switch on the back of the unit. When you flip between them, the screen will update to show you which unit youre currently using. While you shouldnt have to use this very often, its nice to leave the calculator in your desk while you brew coffee.</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image"><img src="https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-base-switches-1024x670.jpg" alt="Switches on the Stagg EKG base" class="wp-image-631" srcset="https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-base-switches-1024x670.jpg 1024w, https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-base-switches-300x196.jpg 300w, https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-base-switches-768x503.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" /></figure>



<h3>Digital Temperature Display</h3>



<p>One of the novel upgrades that the Stagg EKG brought over the Stagg (in addition to being electric) is that it sports a digital temperature display, while the original Stagg uses an analog temperature dial. The EKG lets you achieve incredible precision because it tracks temperature on the digital display to a single degree. We really like being able to see the exact temperature the water is at as it heats up.</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image"><img src="https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-knob-1024x784.jpg" alt="Stagg EKG Digital Display" class="wp-image-630" srcset="https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-knob-1024x784.jpg 1024w, https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-knob-300x230.jpg 300w, https://www.triplebarcoffee.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/EKG-knob-768x588.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" /></figure>



<h3>Holding Temperatures</h3>



<p>Most people brew their coffee right after watching their water heat up. Some of us, however, dont have the patience for that. Luckily, the Stagg EKG is able to hold a set temperature for 60 minutes before shutting off for safety. We dig this feature because we have a tendency to walk off as the water heats up, only to come back to water that has come to room temperature again. Youll never have to heat water twice with the Stagg EKG.</p>



<h3>PID Controller</h3>



<p>One of the more tech-savvy features of the Stagg EKG is its internal PID controller. PID is a fancy electrical engineering acronym for a type of system that reaches a desired setpoint in a very efficient way. The Stagg EKGs PID controller lets it achieve and maintain the temperature setpoint so that you can spend less time waiting on your coffee and more time enjoying it.</p>



<h3>High Power</h3>



<p>Although the kettle base seems small, it is capable of using a shocking 1200 watts of power to heat up your water. This power rivals most espresso machines and will get you from fill-up to brewed cup in no time at all.</p>
